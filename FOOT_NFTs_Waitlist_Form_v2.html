MVP — Launch Now 1️⃣ Core Launch Functionality ▼
  • User Registration & Profiles — Sign up, fan profile, favourite club, participation history
  • Highlight Voting System — Post-match listings, structured voting, momentum tracking, time-limited windows
  • Momentum Threshold Engine — Algorithm (votes + activity + sentiment), public progress bar, threshold unlock
  • "Moment Archived" Announcement — Official confirmation, public archive entry, immutable timestamp
  • Basic Points + Early Voter Badges — Earn for voting, early participation marker, "Historic Contributor" status
Phase 2 2️⃣ Engagement & Identity Layer ▼
  • Fan Participation History — Full record of moments voted on, early voter recognition
  • Advanced Points System — Earn for voting, debates, referrals, engagement streaks
  • Badges — Derby Defender, Early Believer, Club Historian, Momentum Starter, and more
  • Leaderboards — Top contributors per club, weekly ranking, all-time historic voters
  • Fan Reputation Score — Consistency-based long-term identity score
Phase 3 3️⃣ NFT / Archive Layer ▼
  • Blockchain Archival — Highlights permanently stored, immutable metadata, proof of archival on TON
  • Fan-Linked Mint Record — Record of users who voted, timestamped participation proof
  • Highlight Ownership — Limited edition ownership, fan-certified moments, 2.5% platform fee
  • Stakeholder Royalty Share — Club, player, voters and platform all share minting + trading royalties
  • Physical + Digital Jersey Pairing — Highlight embedded into digital twin of physical jersey
Phase 2 — B2B 4️⃣ Sentiment Engine ▼
  • Match Sentiment Capture — Fan emotional response data, structured reaction categories
  • Club Dashboard — Fan mood tracking, highlight popularity, player sentiment analytics, engagement heatmaps
  • Narrative Mapping — Track how a moment evolved pre/post match, longitudinal emotion data
Phase 3 — Infrastructure 5️⃣ Marketplace / Commerce Layer ▼
  • Club Merchandise Listing — Clubs list official merch, verified authenticity. Platform does NOT sell goods directly.
  • Third-Party Vendor Integration — Sports gear sellers onboard, delivery handled by partners
  • Highlight-Linked Merch — Historic highlight jerseys, limited narrative edition kits
  • Secure Transaction Infrastructure — 2.5% commission on highlight-related NFT transactions
Future Vision 6️⃣ Future Expansion Layer ▼
  • Player Highlight Portability — Highlights follow player career across clubs, cross-club narrative tracking
  • Fan Governance — Voting power scales with reputation, proposal system, DAO model
  • Cross-League Expansion — EPL → La Liga → Serie A → UCL → World Cup
  • Historical Back-Minting — Classic matches archived by modern fans, generational voting
  • API for Clubs & Broadcasters — Sentiment API, archive embedding, white-label integration
